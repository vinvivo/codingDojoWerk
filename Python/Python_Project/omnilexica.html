Definition of the adverb a good deal

What does a good deal mean as an attribute of something other than a noun?

adverb

  1. to a very great degree or extent
    • synonyms of a good deal: a great deal / a lot / lots / much / very much

Alternative definition of the adverb a good deal

adverb

  1. [idiomatic] Very much; to a great extent; a lot; lots.

Definition of the noun a good deal

What does a good deal mean as a name of something?

noun

  1. [idiomatic] A large amount; a lot.
